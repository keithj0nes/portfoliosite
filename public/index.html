A little about me

Keith Jones

I was born in the Greater Seattle Area and have never wanted to leave. After feeling stuck in a dead-end job with not a lot of room for growth, I decided to take the leap and attend DevMountain in Provo, Utah. I was a part of their Immersive Web Development course during the Fall of 2016 where we primarly learned the AngularJS Framework, along with NodeJS and PostgreSQL. I get excited at the thought of learning more.


When I'm not working, I spend a lot of time at the local ice rink playing hockey, or on a field playing lacrosse in the Summer.
